Tomato Garlic Spread starter dish

Roasted garlic & tomato spread

This slow-roasted tomato spread makes a tasty addition to any sandwich or a simple appetizer when spread on crostini

Ingredients

  • Garlic: Roasted garlic gives an intense and savoury flavor
  • Tomatoes: Tomatoes give of the juices and fresh taste that will make this spread fantastic
  • Olive oil: Olive oil brings everything together and adds that extra flavor to round it all up

How to prepare it:

  1. Preheat the oven to 120 C
  2. Clean the garlic and tomatoes, toss them in a bowl with olive oil and season to taste
  3. Transfer to a baking tray and place in the oven for 1 hour
  4. Take out of the oven, let it cool down and blend it all together until it's smooth
  5. Serve in a bowl or on top of a bread slice
  6. Enjoy!
